Route 19 Cocktails

Here's how to make our signature cocktails.

Peach Iced Tea

  • 2oz of Ciroc Peach
  • ~2oz lemon bar mix
  • iced tea
  • splash of simple syrup
  • garnish with a lemon slice

Pineapple Sour

  • 1oz Blue Lobster Vodka
  • .5oz Malibu Rum
  • .5oz Sourpuss Raspberry
  • Pineapple juice
  • garnish with a lime slice and a maraschino cherry

Pink Lemonade

  • 1oz Blue Lobster Vodka
  • 1oz Pink Whitney
  • One half lemonade, one half 7up
  • Topped with grenadine
  • Garnished with a lemon slice and a maraschino cherry

Classic Sling

  • 1oz Willing to Learn Gin
  • 1oz Triple Sec
  • ~1.5oz lemon bar mix
  • Soda water
  • Top with Grenadine and McGuinness Cherry Brandy
  • Garnish with an orange slice and a maraschino cherry

Moscow Mule

  • 2oz Blue Lobster Vodka
  • Ginger Beer
  • ~1oz Lime Cordial, or lime juice
  • Serve in a copper mug garnished with a lime slice

Route 19 Caesar

  • 2oz Blue Lobster Vodka
  • 4 dashes of Tabasco sauce
  • 4 dashes of Worchestershire sauce
  • Splash of pickled bean juice
  • Mott's Clamato Caesar mix
  • Garnish with a spicy bean, a pepperoni stick, and a lemon slice
  • Serve in a glass rimmed with calery salt
